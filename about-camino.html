Camino

Camino is an endless walk along the narrow way. Swipe to keep the pilgrim on the path — around the rocks, across the water — and see how far the road carries you.

The name comes from the Camino de Santiago, the Way of St James: a pilgrim road walked for over a thousand years, marked by the scallop shell its walkers carry as a sign of the journey. Here the road never ends — there is only the walking, and how far you are willing to go.
